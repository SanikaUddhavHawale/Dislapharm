Some users may experience slight nausea, digestive discomfort, or mild headaches. These effects are usually temporary as the body adjusts.

Age Restriction

ChronoNAD+ is not recommended for individuals under 18 years of age. Younger bodies have different metabolic needs and safety data is limited.

Pregnancy & Nursing

Avoid use during pregnancy or breastfeeding unless prescribed by a doctor. Safety during these stages has not been clinically established.

Medical Conditions

Consult your physician before use if you have chronic illnesses or are taking medications. This ensures safe compatibility with your treatment.

Allergy Check

Always review the ingredient list carefully to avoid allergic reactions. Stop use immediately if any adverse symptoms occur.
